# Prompt-Quest

Welcome to Prompt-Quest, an interactive web application designed to spark your creativity and imagination! With Prompt-Quest, you can engage in fun and challenging prompts that inspire you to think outside the box and explore your creative potential.

## Features

- Choose from various categories including car, place, food, and animal.
- Receive prompts tailored to each category.
- Express your creativity by responding to prompts in your preferred medium.
- Share your creations with the Prompt-Quest community.
- Collaborate and connect with other players.
